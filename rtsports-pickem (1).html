How Pick'em Works
Pick players to go MORE or LESS than their projection — it's that simple.
1
Choose your players
Browse players across NBA, MLB, NHL and more. Each player has a projected stat line set by our analytics team — updated up to 30 minutes before game time.
2
Pick MORE or LESS
For each player, decide whether they'll finish MORE (above) or LESS (below) their projection. You need at least 2 picks and can go up to 6.
3
Choose Power Play or Flex
Power Play — all picks must hit for the full multiplier. Higher risk, higher reward.

Flex — if one pick misses on a 3–6 pick slip, you still get a reduced payout instead of losing everything.
4
Enter your amount & play
Set your entry amount (min $1, max $100). Your payout is your entry × the multiplier for your pick count. Entries lock when the first game on your slip starts.

Donovan Mitchell ▲ MORE 24.5 Points
Jalen Duren ▲ MORE 12.5 Rebounds
LeBron James ▼ LESS 7.5 Assists
